## 4. PPT Structure & Content
**Total Slides: 15‚Äì25** (Solo ‚â§20; Duo ‚â§25). **Font ‚â•24pt; ‚â§5 lines/slide.**

| Section              | Key Content                                                                 | Slides | Time     |
|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------|----------|
| **1. Title & Intro** | Background, Motivation, **3‚Äì5 Contributions**<br/>Visualize problem (e.g., pain points chart) | 2‚Äì3    | 3 min   |
| **2. Related Work**  | Baselines + Positioning (**Why ICLR?**)<br/>**Comparison Table**            | 2      | 3 min   |
| **3. Method (Core)** | **Detailed:** Math, Algo Flow, **Visuals (Arch/Pseudo-code)**<br/>Duo: Split (Deriv. vs. Impl.) | 6‚Äì8    | 8‚Äì10 min |
| **4. Experiments**   | Datasets, Metrics, **Main Tables/Figs**, Ablations<br/>**Highlight SOTA in color**; Explain "Why it works" | 4‚Äì6    | 6‚Äì8 min |
| **5. Discussion & Limits** | Pros/Cons, **Real Impact**, Future<br/>**Critical:** e.g., "High compute?" | 2      | 3 min   |
| **6. Conclusion & Q&A** | Summary + **3 Thought Questions**                                      | 1      | 2 min   |

**Design Tips:**
- **80% visuals/equations, 20% text.**
- Use **animations** for complex diagrams.
